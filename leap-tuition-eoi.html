Why families choose LEAP

Welcome to LEAP Tuition & Early Learning, where every child is met with warmth, structure, and high expectations. We believe learning should build real confidence — not just tick off a curriculum.

LEAP brings a teacher-led Prep School and personalised tutoring together under one roof, so families have a trusted place to grow — from age three right through to Year 12.
